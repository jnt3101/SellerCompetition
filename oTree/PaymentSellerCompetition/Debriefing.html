Purpose of this study

The purpose of this study was to examine how key features of loot boxes in lotteries offered by other participants influence the willingness to pay for subjects. You were part of the buying subjects and stated your willingness to pay for five lotteries. We want to link overspending on loot boxes to problematic gambling behavior.

Concerns and Discomfort

During the study, we also asked you about your gambling behavior. If gambling is causing problems for you, you can seek assistance in treatment and support groups. For more information, please visit the NHS page.
